# PTO Policy

Full-time Northwind Cloud employees accrue paid time off (PTO) at a rate of 20 days per year, accrued monthly at a rate of 1.67 days per month starting from the first day of employment — there is no waiting period before accrual begins.

PTO can be used for vacation, personal time, or short-term illness. Employees should request PTO through the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance for planned absences; same-day requests for illness are accepted without penalty.

At the end of the calendar year, employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the following year. Any unused PTO beyond that 5-day carryover cap is paid out at the employee's current daily rate in the December payroll cycle. Carried-over days must be used by March 31 of the following year or they are forfeited (they are not paid out a second time).

PTO is separate from the parental leave policy described in `parental-leave.md`, which is a distinct, additional benefit and does not draw down the standard PTO balance. Company holidays (a fixed list of 10 per year, published on the HR portal) are also separate from PTO and do not need to be requested.
