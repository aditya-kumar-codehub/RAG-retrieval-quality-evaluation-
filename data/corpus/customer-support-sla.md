# Customer Support SLA

Support response-time commitments are tied to pricing tier, as introduced in `pricing-tiers.md`:

- **Free**: community forum only, no formal response-time SLA.
- **Starter**: email support, 24-hour first-response SLA during business days.
- **Pro**: live chat support, 4-hour first-response SLA, 7 days a week.
- **Enterprise**: dedicated support channel (Slack Connect or a shared support queue), 1-hour first-response SLA, 24/7, plus a named customer success manager.

These SLAs cover first response time, not time-to-resolution — complex issues may take longer to resolve but will receive an initial acknowledgment within the stated window. SLA credits are available to Pro and Enterprise customers if Northwind Cloud misses the committed first-response window on a documented Sev1 or Sev2 support ticket; credits are issued as a percentage of that month's subscription fee, calculated on a sliding scale based on how far the response exceeded the SLA.

Support tickets related to active platform incidents are handled through the incident process described in `incident-response.md` rather than the standard support queue, and are automatically prioritized above the SLA commitments listed here.
