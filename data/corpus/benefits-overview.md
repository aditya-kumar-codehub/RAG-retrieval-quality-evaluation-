# Benefits Overview

Northwind Cloud covers 100% of the employee's health insurance premium and 75% of the premium for enrolled dependents, across medical, dental, and vision plans. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following the employee's start date.

The company offers a 401(k) retirement plan with a 4% match, dollar-for-dollar, vested immediately with no cliff — matched contributions belong to the employee from day one. Enrollment is available at any time, not just during a fixed open-enrollment window.

Other standard benefits include the equipment and PTO policies described in `equipment-policy.md` and `pto-policy.md`, respectively, as well as the parental leave benefit in `parental-leave.md`. Employees also receive a $1,000 annual professional development budget for courses, books, or conference attendance, which is separate from the general expense reimbursement process in `expense-policy.md` and does not require department-head approval regardless of amount, up to the $1,000 cap.

Life insurance (1x annual salary) and short-term disability coverage are provided automatically at no cost to the employee.
