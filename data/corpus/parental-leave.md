# Parental Leave Policy

Northwind Cloud offers paid parental leave to all full-time employees regardless of tenure, effective from the first day of employment. The primary caregiver receives 16 weeks of fully paid leave; the secondary caregiver receives 8 weeks of fully paid leave. Leave can be taken continuously or split into two blocks within the first 12 months following birth, adoption, or foster placement, subject to manager approval on timing.

Parental leave is a distinct benefit from standard PTO (`pto-policy.md`) and does not draw down an employee's PTO balance. It also does not count against the 90-day probationary period referenced in `remote-work-policy.md` for hybrid-to-remote transitions — that clock pauses during leave and resumes upon return.

Health insurance coverage, described in `benefits-overview.md`, continues uninterrupted during parental leave at the same employee cost as while actively working. Employees intending to take parental leave should notify HR and their manager at least 30 days before the expected start date where possible, though Northwind Cloud accommodates shorter notice for unplanned circumstances such as premature birth.
