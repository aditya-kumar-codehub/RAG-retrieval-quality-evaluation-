# Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees may submit business expenses for reimbursement through the finance portal. Expenses under $500 can be self-approved by the employee's direct manager with a single click; expenses of $500 or more require additional approval from a department head before reimbursement is processed.

All expense claims must include an itemized receipt; a credit card statement line alone is not sufficient documentation. Claims must be submitted within 60 days of the expense being incurred — claims submitted after 60 days are not guaranteed reimbursement and require a documented exception from finance.

Approved reimbursements are processed within 30 days of submission, paid out via direct deposit alongside the next regular payroll cycle after approval.

Common reimbursable categories include the remote work equipment stipend (`remote-work-policy.md`), business travel and per diem (`travel-policy.md`), professional development (courses, conference tickets, books relevant to the employee's role), and client entertainment (with a $150 per-person cap). Alcohol is reimbursable only in the context of client entertainment and is not reimbursable for personal or team-only meals.
