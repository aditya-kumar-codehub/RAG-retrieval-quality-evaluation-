# Business Travel Policy

All business travel must be booked through the company travel portal to ensure it's captured for duty-of-care and expense-tracking purposes; booking directly with an airline or hotel and submitting for reimbursement afterward is discouraged and requires manager pre-approval.

Flights under 6 hours in duration are booked in economy class. Flights of 6 hours or longer, including international long-haul flights, may be booked in premium economy or business class at the traveler's discretion, subject to the fare being reasonable for the route.

Per diem rates for meals and incidentals not otherwise covered are $75 per day for domestic travel and $100 per day for international travel; these are flat rates and do not require itemized receipts, unlike the general expense categories described in `expense-policy.md`. Hotel costs are booked directly through the travel portal and are not subject to a per-night cap, but should reflect a reasonable business rate for the destination.

Travel expenses are reimbursed through the same process and 30-day timeline described in `expense-policy.md`, with the exception of the flat per diem, which is paid automatically based on the trip's booked dates without requiring a separate claim.
