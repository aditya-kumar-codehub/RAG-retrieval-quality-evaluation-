# Equipment Policy

Every employee is issued a standard-configuration laptop (currently a MacBook Pro 14") on their start date, shipped to arrive before day one for remote employees. Laptops are refreshed every 3 years, or sooner if hardware fails and is beyond economical repair.

All company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled and are enrolled in mobile device management (MDM) prior to shipping, per the requirements in `security-policy.md`. Employees may not disable MDM enrollment or encryption at any point during their employment.

Employees who prefer a non-standard configuration (a different laptop model, additional monitors beyond the two included in the remote equipment stipend, etc.) may request an exception through IT, subject to a cost cap of $500 above the standard configuration price, which the employee's department must approve.

Upon offboarding, all company equipment must be returned within 10 business days via a prepaid shipping label provided by IT. Equipment not returned within 30 days is billed to the departing employee at replacement cost.
