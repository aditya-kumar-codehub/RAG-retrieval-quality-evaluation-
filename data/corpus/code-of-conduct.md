# Code of Conduct

Northwind Cloud has a zero-tolerance policy toward harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind, applying equally to interactions with colleagues, customers, and vendors. This applies both in the workplace and at company-sponsored events, whether in-person or virtual.

Employees who witness or experience a violation can report it through three channels: directly to their manager, to any member of the People team, or anonymously through the third-party ethics hotline linked on the HR portal. Reports made through the ethics hotline are handled by an external firm and are not visible to Northwind Cloud management unless the reporter chooses to be identified.

All reports are investigated within 10 business days of receipt. Retaliation against anyone who makes a good-faith report, even if the underlying complaint is not substantiated, is itself a violation of this policy and is grounds for termination.

The Code of Conduct is reviewed and re-acknowledged annually by all employees as part of the security and compliance training described in `security-policy.md`.
