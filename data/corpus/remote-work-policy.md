# Remote Work Policy

Northwind Cloud is a remote-first company. New employees are eligible to work fully remote starting on day one for roles explicitly designated "remote," and after a 90-day probationary period for roles designated "hybrid" that later transition to remote by manager approval.

Employees must reside in a state or country where Northwind Cloud has an active tax registration in order to be paid and to legally work remotely from that location. As of this writing, Northwind Cloud is registered to employ workers in the following US states: California, New York, Texas, Washington, Colorado, and Massachusetts, as well as in Canada and the United Kingdom. Employees relocating to a new state or country must notify HR at least 30 days in advance so tax registration can be confirmed or established.

Remote employees receive a one-time $500 equipment stipend to set up a home office (desk, chair, monitor, etc.), separate from the standard company laptop described in `equipment-policy.md`. The stipend is paid via expense reimbursement following the process in `expense-policy.md` and must be claimed within the first 90 days of employment.

Employees are expected to maintain a reliable internet connection and to be available during their team's core collaboration hours, typically a 4-hour overlap window defined by each manager.
